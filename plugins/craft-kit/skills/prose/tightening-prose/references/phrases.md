# Phrases to Cut — technical-doc register

The phrase-level half of the pass. The full register-agnostic catalog — throat-clearing, emphasis crutches, business jargon, empty adverbs and filler, meta-commentary, telling-instead-of-showing, vague declaratives — lives in [phrase-catalog.md](../../shared/phrase-catalog.md). Apply all of it.

**Register exception (technical docs).** From the empty-adverb list, cut only the *empty* intensifiers and softeners; KEEP adverbs that change meaning ("atomically", "lazily", "concurrently") and hedges that mark genuine uncertainty ("likely", "approximately", "unverified"). The full carve-out and the structural patterns: [structures.md](./structures.md); worked before/after, including a leave-it-alone technical case: [examples.md](../assets/examples.md).
