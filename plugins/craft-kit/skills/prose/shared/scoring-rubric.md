# Scoring Rubric — 5 dimensions

Shared by `tightening-prose` and `humanizing-prose`. A heavier check than the lightweight final pass — use it when a piece is going out and you want a number.

| Dimension | High score means |
| --- | --- |
| Directness | states the point; no throat-clearing, no hedging, no rhetorical setup |
| Rhythm | sentence length varies naturally; no monotone, no forced staccato |
| Trust | trusts the reader; no over-explaining, no manufactured suspense |
| Authenticity | a human voice; no performative sincerity, no manufactured emphasis |
| Density | every sentence earns its place; no filler |

Sum the five. **Below 35/50: revise.**

**Weighting is register-dependent.** In a **technical** register weight Directness and Density highest — Rhythm and Authenticity matter less for a spec than for an essay. In a **narrative / article** register weight Authenticity and Rhythm highest — they are what a reader registers as "a person wrote this", and exactly what the technical register weights lowest.
