# Phrases to Cut — narrative / article register

The phrase-level half of the pass. The full register-agnostic catalog — throat-clearing, emphasis crutches, business jargon, empty adverbs and filler, meta-commentary, telling-instead-of-showing, vague declaratives — lives in [phrase-catalog.md](../../shared/phrase-catalog.md). Apply all of it.

**Register note (narrative).** Unlike a technical doc there are NO carve-outs for terse density — cut the empty adverbs and filler freely, keeping only a hedge that carries genuine uncertainty ("likely", "roughly") when the honesty matters. The structural tells specific to this register (em-dash contrasts, dramatic fragments, binary-contrast crutch) and the register-inversion table live in [narrative-tells.md](./narrative-tells.md); worked before/after in [examples.md](../assets/examples.md).
